Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Introduction

TheDeepVerse ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website at thedeepverse.netlify.app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Email Address: When you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Contact Form Data: Name, email, subject, and message when you contact us.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage data such as pages visited, browser type, device type, and approximate location (country level) through Google Analytics.
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device for analytics and site functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you our weekly psychology newsletter (only if you subscribed).
  • To respond to your inquiries submitted through the contact form.
  • To understand how visitors use our site and improve our content.
  • To ensure the security and performance of our website.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous website traffic analysis. Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree: For processing contact form submissions. Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube (Google): For embedding videos. YouTube may collect data when you interact with embedded videos.
  • Google Fonts: For loading web fonts. Google may log your IP address.

5. Cookies

We use cookies for analytics purposes. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality but will not prevent you from accessing content.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in every email.
  • Request deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact form.
