Refund Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Digital Products (eBooks, AI Prompts, Templates)

Due to the nature of digital goods, all sales of digital products are generally considered final. However, we offer a 7-day refund policy under strict conditions. If you purchase a digital product and have not downloaded or accessed the files, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your purchase.

Once a digital product has been downloaded or accessed, it becomes strictly non-refundable. Our systems log download activity, and this data will be used to verify eligibility for a refund.

Courses

Our online courses come with a conditional 7-day money-back guarantee. If you are not satisfied with the course, you may request a refund within 7 days of purchase, provided you have viewed less than 20% of the course content. Refunds will not be granted if a significant portion of the course has been completed or resources have been downloaded.

Payment Processing and Timelines

All approved refunds will be processed through our payment gateway, Razorpay. The refund will be credited back to the original method of payment used during the transaction.

  • Domestic refunds (India) typically take 5-7 business days to reflect in your account.
  • International refunds may take 7-14 business days, depending on your bank and country.
  • Any currency conversion fees or foreign transaction fees charged by your bank are non-refundable.

How to Request a Refund

  • Submit a request via our Contact page.
  • Include your order number, the email address used for the purchase, and a brief explanation of why you are requesting a refund.
  • Our team will review your request and notify you of the approval or rejection within 48 hours.

Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding our Refund Policy, please reach out to us via our Contact page.
